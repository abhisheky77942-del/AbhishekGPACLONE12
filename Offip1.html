About Government Polytechnic Adityapur

Government Polytechnic, Adityapur, established in 1980, is a premier state-government technical institute under the Department of Higher Technical Education & Skill Development, Government of Jharkhand. [ more info...]

  • State-of-the-art laboratories and workshops
  • Industry-focused diploma programmes

To impart quality technical education with strong practical exposure, develop skilled and ethical professionals, and meet the evolving requirements of industry and society.

  • Emphasis on hands-on technical skills
  • Holistic student development

To emerge as a centre of excellence in diploma engineering education, producing competent and responsible professionals capable of contributing to national and industrial growth.

  • Industry-ready and socially responsible graduates
  • Continuous improvement in academic standards

Why Choose Government Polytechnic Adityapur

Placement Support

Consistent placement support with participation from reputed core and IT sector industries.

Affordable Education

State-funded technical education with highly affordable fees for all categories.

Scholarship Facilities

Laboratories & Library

Hostel & Campus Facilities

Hostel accommodation for boys and girls with essential campus infrastructure.
